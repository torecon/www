Privacy Policy

Updated: May 2026 · evChargeScope for macOS and iOS

Summary: evChargeScope stores your charging data locally on your device. On iOS and macOS, data is optionally synced via Apple iCloud between your own devices — fully under your control. No personal data is transmitted to us or to third parties. No account with torecon, no tracking.

1. Data Controller

The controller in the sense of the GDPR is:

Thomas Reinke · torecon
Roesgenstr. 22

E-mail: info@torecon.de

2. Data we do NOT collect

evChargeScope does not require a user account or registration. We do not collect:

  • Names, e-mail addresses, or other identifiers
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Camera, microphone, or contact access
  • Social network data
  • Payment data (handled exclusively by Apple)

3. Data stored on your device

evChargeScope stores the following data locally on your Mac or iPhone (SQLite database):

  • Charging sessions (date, odometer reading, provider, price, energy, notes)
  • Vehicle profiles (name, battery capacity, target consumption)
  • Providers and tariff models
  • App settings (accent color, tile background, CO₂ parameters) in UserDefaults
  • Active promo code (e-mail + code) in the local database

This data leaves your device only in the following cases: (a) you export it actively (e.g. as a PDF or database file), or (b) iCloud sync is active (see section 4a).

4a. iCloud sync (optional)

On iOS and macOS, evChargeScope uses Apple iCloud Drive to automatically sync your database file between your own Apple devices. The rules are:

  • Sync only happens between devices signed in with the same Apple ID.
  • Data is stored and transmitted via Apple iCloud — fully under your control as the Apple account holder.
  • We (torecon) have no access to your iCloud data.
  • iCloud sync is subject to Apple's privacy policy.
  • You can disable iCloud usage at any time in iOS or macOS system settings under "iCloud → evChargeScope".

4b. In-App purchases (StoreKit 2)

evChargeScope offers optional subscriptions and a one-time purchase via Apple. Payment processing, invoicing, and refunds are handled exclusively by Apple Inc. (USA) according to Apple's privacy policy. We do not receive any payment data or personal information.

For payment questions, please contact Apple directly via your device's system settings.

5. Website (chargescope.ai)

When you visit our website, the web server stores the following data in server log files:

  • IP address (anonymized; deleted after 7 days)
  • Date and time of access
  • Requested URL
  • Browser and operating system (User-Agent)
  • Referrer URL (if available)

This data is used only to ensure operations. No tracking cookies are set.

6. Third-party services

The evChargeScope app communicates with our own server at downloads.torecon.de for update checks. No personal data is transmitted — the server only returns the current version number. Connection data (IP address, timestamp) is stored in server logs for a maximum of 7 days.

Beyond that, evChargeScope only communicates with Apple services (App Store, StoreKit) according to Apple's privacy policy.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

Because evChargeScope does not collect or store personal data, we do not hold any data about you that we could provide, correct, or delete. Your charging data is fully under your control on your device.

For requests regarding website log data, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)

Contact: info@torecon.de

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent data protection supervisory authority. For Rhineland-Palatinate: www.datenschutz.rlp.de

8. Data security

The local SQLite database of evChargeScope lives under macOS in ~/Library/Application Support/eLadung/, protected by the macOS App Sandbox. On iOS, the database file is stored in the app sandbox directory. During iCloud sync, the file is transmitted encrypted by Apple and stored in your personal iCloud Drive. Website connections are HTTPS/TLS only.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as needed. The current version is always available at chargescope.ai/privacy/.
