2. Description of services

evChargeScope is an application for macOS and iOS to record and analyze charging costs for electric vehicles. The app includes:

  • Logging charging sessions (date, odometer reading, provider, price, energy)
  • Dashboard with KPI tiles, monthly charts, and provider comparison
  • Management of vehicle profiles and tariff models
  • Economic analysis and tariff comparison
  • PDF export of statistics
  • Local data storage with optional iCloud sync between your own devices

The app is provided as a single-user license for personal use on Apple devices linked to your Apple account.
